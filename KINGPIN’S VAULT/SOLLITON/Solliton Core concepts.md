> [!tip]+ TIps:
> Attractive force:
> 
> Gravity always pulls objects toward each other, never pushes.
> So m₁ pulls m₂, and m₂ pulls m₁.
> 
> Equal and opposite forces:
> 
> The force on m₁ due to m₂ = the force on m₂ due to m₁
> Directions are opposite (Newton’s 3rd law), magnitudes are same.
> 
> Acts along the line joining them:
> 
> That dashed straight line between m₁ and m₂ is the line of action of gravity.
> 
> Distance matters:
> 
> The farther apart they are (larger r), the weaker the force.
